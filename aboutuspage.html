Motto

JXU's school motto of "seeking truth from facts and
applying practicality" is a combination of geography, popularity
and the school's history of running a school.

School Profile

The school adheres to the educational concept of "giving the right to
become a talent to students" and implements the reform of the talent training model of
"big-category enrollment and big-category training".
